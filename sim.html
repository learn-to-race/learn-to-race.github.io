Obtaining the Arrival Racing Simulator

The Arrival Autonomous Racing Simulator is currently available to the research community, under an academic-use license. To obtain the simulator, please complete the license agreement and the online request form.

While access to the simulator is obtained, please review our Learn-to-Race code and documentation.
